Shipping & Delivery

At JNJ Auto Sales, we ensure your classic car is delivered with the utmost care, professionalism, and attention to detail—just as it deserves.

1. Delivery Options

  • White-Glove Enclosed Transport: Climate-controlled, fully insured carriers for maximum protection.
  • Open-Air Transport: Cost-effective option for local or regional delivery.
  • International Shipping: Door-to-door service with customs handling and documentation.

2. Delivery Timeline

Delivery typically takes 7–14 business days after purchase confirmation, depending on destination and transport method. Remote or international locations may require additional time.

3. Insurance & Protection

All vehicles are fully insured during transit. We partner with premium auto transporters who specialize in classic and luxury vehicles.

4. Delivery Process

  • Coordinate with you to schedule delivery.
  • Provide real-time tracking updates.
  • Ensure the vehicle is inspected upon arrival.
  • Assist with unloading and final handover.

5. Delivery Fees

Fees are calculated based on distance, vehicle size, and service level. A detailed quote will be provided before shipping.

6. Shipping Process Steps

1

Purchase Confirmation

Once your vehicle is purchased, we initiate the shipping process.

2

Transport Arrangement

3

Real-Time Tracking

You’ll receive updates throughout transit.

4

Final Delivery

Professional unloading and handover at your location.
